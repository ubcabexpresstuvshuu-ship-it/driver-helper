# UBCAB Express — Brand instructions

## Logo
- Official logo file: `assets/ubcab-express-logo.png` (1074×283, transparent background).
- ALWAYS use this exact logo file. Never redraw, recreate, or approximate it in SVG/CSS.
- **Preserve aspect ratio** (240:73 ≈ 3.29:1). Never stretch, squash, or distort. Scale proportionally only.
- Keep the logo at high quality / crisp. Give it clear space and never place it on a busy or low-contrast background.

## Brand color
- Primary brand color is the **orange** from the logo: **#F58220** (rgb 245, 130, 32).
- Use this orange tone as the primary accent across all presentations and materials.
- Pair with neutral darks/whites; keep the orange as the figure/accent color.

## Notes
- Project: UBCAB Express. Presentations and materials should follow this brand (orange #F58220 + the logo).
